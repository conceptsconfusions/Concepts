Net Neutrality

I wrote this in 2016. The situation in Canada has changed for the better in recent months. In America the situation has grown worse. The future of the internet will be decided in a debate over the identity of Internet Service Providers (ISPs). On one side, the ISPs remain as their name describes, service providers and passive members in the functioning of the internet.
